```
Health Plan Factory gives you a personalized wellness plan built around your real life — your budget, your goals, and your health conditions. Not generic advice. Your plan.

Tell us what you're working on — back pain, stress, sleep, energy, weight — and your monthly wellness budget. Our AI assembles a prioritized plan across proven modalities like massage, acupuncture, yoga, physical therapy, and nutrition coaching. Then we match you to vetted providers near you who specialize in exactly what's in your plan.

WHAT YOU GET WITH PLUS
• Your personalized wellness plan — updated whenever your goals change
• AI accountability coach — tracks your sessions, nudges your habits, celebrates progress
• Journal & session log — rate how you feel after every session, spot what's working
• Wellness score & insights — see which modalities are actually moving your numbers
• Full provider directory — contact info, bios, and specialties for every matched provider
• HSA/FSA spending log — track tax-advantaged wellness spending in one place
• Unlimited plan revisions — life changes; your plan should too

HOW IT WORKS
1. Tell us about you — Share your goals, conditions, monthly budget, and preferences. Takes about 5 minutes.
2. Get your plan — Your AI plan arrives instantly, prioritized by evidence strength and budget fit. Every modality includes a rationale, typical cost, and recommended frequency.
3. Find your providers — Browse matched providers in your area. Contact them directly through the app to book.

WHY HEALTH PLAN FACTORY IS DIFFERENT
Most wellness apps tell you what to do. We tell you what to spend — and who to spend it with. Every modality recommendation is tagged with its evidence level (Strong / Moderate / Emerging) so you know the difference between a well-researched intervention and an experiment. We don't sell supplements, push affiliate products, or take commissions from providers on bookings. Our only revenue is your subscription.
```
