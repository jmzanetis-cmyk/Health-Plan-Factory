What HealthPlanFactory is — and isn't

We believe transparency builds trust. Here's exactly what you're getting, and what you're not.

Included HealthPlanFactory IS

  • A wellness optimization and care navigation platform
  • A tool to help you understand your options across 47+ modalities
  • A budget-aware plan builder that ranks recommendations by evidence

  • A complement to your existing medical care

Not included HealthPlanFactory is NOT

  • A licensed medical provider or healthcare organization
  • A diagnostic tool — we do not identify or name medical conditions
  • A treatment provider — we do not prescribe, cure, or treat anything
  • A replacement for your physician, specialist, or therapist
  • A mental health crisis service — call 988 for crisis support
  • A guarantee that any modality will work for your specific situation
  • A source of medical advice, diagnosis, or clinical opinion
Always keep your physician informed of your wellness activities. HealthPlanFactory is designed to complement your care — never to replace conversations with your doctor, specialist, or licensed mental health provider. Notice Medical Disclaimer & Important Notices

Important Medical Emergency: If you are experiencing a medical emergency, stop and call 911 immediately. HealthPlanFactory cannot assist with emergencies and is not a substitute for emergency services.
MB Mental Health Crisis: If you or someone you know is in mental health crisis, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line). These services are free and available 24/7.

Not a Medical Provider

HealthPlanFactory is a wellness optimization and care navigation platform. It is not a licensed medical provider, hospital, clinic, or healthcare organization. HealthPlanFactory does not employ physicians, nurses, therapists, or any licensed clinical staff in connection with your wellness plan.

Not Medical Advice

Nothing on HealthPlanFactory — including AI-generated wellness plans, provider recommendations, or any written content — constitutes medical advice, a clinical opinion, a diagnosis, or a treatment plan. All content is for informational and wellness planning purposes only.

Not a Diagnostic Tool

HealthPlanFactory does not diagnose medical conditions. We ask about your health picture to personalize your wellness plan — not to assess, identify, or name any medical condition. If you believe you have a medical condition, consult a licensed healthcare provider.

Consult Your Physician

Always consult your physician or licensed healthcare provider before beginning any new wellness regimen, especially if you have a chronic condition, are pregnant, are taking prescription medications, or have recently had surgery or an injury. Keep your provider informed of all wellness activities.

Provider Relationships

Wellness providers and physicians listed on HealthPlanFactory are independent practitioners. HealthPlanFactory is a referral marketplace only — it does not supervise, direct, employ, or guarantee the services of any listed provider. The professional relationship is between you and your chosen provider directly.

HSA / FSA Eligibility

Some wellness services may be eligible for reimbursement through HSA or FSA accounts depending on your specific plan. HealthPlanFactory uses the word "may be eligible" intentionally — eligibility is not guaranteed and depends on your plan administrator's determination. A Letter of Medical Necessity from a licensed physician may be required. Consult your plan administrator or tax advisor before making HSA/FSA claims.

AI-Generated Plans

Wellness plans on HealthPlanFactory are generated by AI based on the information you provide. AI outputs are for wellness guidance only and are not reviewed or approved by licensed medical professionals before delivery. Results may vary. HealthPlanFactory does not warrant that any AI recommendation is appropriate for your specific medical situation.
